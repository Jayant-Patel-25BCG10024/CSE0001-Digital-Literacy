Project Overview
This repository serves as my final project for the Digital Literacy course. In the role of a **Student Digital Ambassador**, I have curated this portfolio to help my peers navigate the digital landscape professionally, safely, and effectively.

The project is divided into five core modules, ranging from digital content creation to cyber safety.

---

Modules Summaries

* Module 1: Digital Presentation
The Goal: Show you can communicate ideas visually.

* Module 2: Professional Portfolio
The Goal: Build your online identity.

Action: Set up a LinkedIn profile if you haven't. Add your VIT Bhopal details. Take a screenshot of your "About" section and your profile header.

* Module 3: Digital Platforms
The Goal: Prove you can use tools for learning.

Action: Take screenshots of your VITyarthi dashboard, or perhaps a Coursera course you've started. Show that you know how to navigate these interfaces.

* Module 4: Email Etiquette
The Goal: Write like a professional, not a "texter."

Checklist for your sample email:

    1. Clear Subject Line.

    2. Formal Salutation (e.g., "Dear Professor...").

    3. Concise body text.

    4. Professional sign-off (Name, Reg No, Branch).

* Module 5: Cybercrime & Safety
The Goal: Awareness of digital threats.

Case Study: Research a simple "Phishing" scam where someone lost access to an account. Explain what happened and provide 3 tips to prevent it (e.g., Enabling 2FA).
